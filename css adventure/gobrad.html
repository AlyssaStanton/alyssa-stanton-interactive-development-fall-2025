Gobrad the Healer

Gobrad left his village of Goblins at a young age to seek adventure. In his travels, he discovered an affinity for helping and healing others. As magic is frowned upon by Goblin-kind, he finally left one day, never to return. He set up shop in the old village, and has become this side of the world's most famously known healer since.
