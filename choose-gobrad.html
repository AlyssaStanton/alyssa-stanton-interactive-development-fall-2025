    never to return, after dedicating his life to helping people and becoming a healer, something which is heavily forbidden by 
    most goblin kind. He quickly became the most renowned healer in the small village he relocated to, and word of his miracles 
    spread across the lands. Gobrad is now visited by all kinds of scholars and people in need of healing from all over the world.
