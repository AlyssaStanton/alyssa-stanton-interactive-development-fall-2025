This is Gobrad. He is the most popular goblin in town. Gobrad disappeared from his goblin village without a trace, never to return after dedicating his life to helping people and becoming a healer, something which is forbidden by most goblin kind. He soon became the most renowned healer in the small village he relocated to, and word of his miracles spread across the lands. Gobrad is now visited by all kinds of scholars and people in need of healing from all over the world.
