About Me

Hi, I'm Alyssa!

I'm a passionate graphic design student with a keen interest in UX/UI design, package design, and advertising! I am a proud current student of the University of Arkansas in Fort Smith. Throughout my degree program in Graphic Design, I've developed a strong foundation in visual communication and found a specific niche in advertisement, branding, and web design. I have developed skills among a variety different areas such as photography, video editing, and coding as well. I am actively working as a Graphic Designer doing advertising work for local businesses, as well.

In my time not spent designing or working, I enjoy playing and discovering new video games, traveling to new places, and spending time with my friends and family. Designing and creating is something I am very passionate about overall, and I take pride in my designs. I look forward to expanding my skills and knowledge towards my design career, and am excited to see where it takes me.
